    SHATTERED – Quick Reference Sheet

    Sequence of Play

    Phase Summary
    1. Recovery Stunned → Knocked Down. Resolve start-of-turn effects.
    2. Movement Move, Run, Charge, Jump, or Stand Up.
    3. Shooting Eligible models resolve ranged attacks.
    4. Close Combat All Engaged models fight.
    5. Rout Check Rout threshold. Take Rout Test if required.

    Players alternate activating their warband during each phase. No reactions. No interrupts.

    Movement

    Action Distance Restrictions
    Normal Move M Cannot move through models or impassable terrain.
    Run M + I Cannot shoot. Cannot be Engaged at start of activation.
    Charge M + I Must reach base contact. Strikes first if successful.
    • Pre-measuring is permitted.
    • A model that cannot legally complete a move stops at the furthest legal point.

    Shooting

    Eligibility

    A model may shoot if it did not Run, did not Charge, and is not Engaged. Assault weapons may always fire unless otherwise prohibited.

    To-Hit Modifiers

    Condition Modifier
    Firing model moved (non-Assault weapon) –1 to hit
    Target beyond half range –1 to hit
    Soft cover (partially obscured, or within 1" of another model) –1 to hit
    Hard cover (mostly obscured by solid terrain) –2 to hit

    Modifiers stack. A model that cannot be seen cannot be targeted.

    Ammunition

    Roll at the start of battle, once per weapon.

    Boltguns — roll 1D6:

    D6 Result
    1 Unstable Supply — unmodified hit roll of 1 triggers Misfire.
    2–5 That many shots for the battle.
    6 Unlimited shots.

    Special Weapons — roll 1D3:

    D3 Result
    1 One shot.
    2 Two shots.
    3 Three shots.
    • Bolt Pistols are not subject to ammunition scarcity.
    • Weapons with FP > 2 still count as a single shot.
    • Any unmodified hit roll of 1 with a Special Weapon triggers a Misfire.

    Misfire Table — roll 1D6

    D6 Result
    1 BOOM! S4 hit on shooter (no crit possible). Weapon destroyed.
    2 Jammed. Weapon useless for the rest of the battle.
    3 Phut. Cannot fire this weapon next turn.
    4–5 Click. Fails to fire, no further effect.
    6 KA-BOOM! Hits intended target at +1 Strength.

    Melee

    Charging

    • Declare Charge → move up to M + I → must reach base contact.
    • Charging model strikes first in the first round of combat.
    • A model may not Charge if it began its activation Engaged.

    Shield Bash (Boarding Shield only)

    May be declared if the charger would reach base contact with more than 2" of movement remaining.

    Result Effect
    Charger wins Push target up to 2" directly away. Target falls if displaced off a ledge; otherwise Knocked Down. Charger retains strike priority.
    Tie or charger loses Shield Bash fails. Defender strikes first.

    Combat Sequence

    1. Determine strike order (chargers first).
    2. Roll To Hit (WS vs WS).
    3. Roll To Wound.
    4. Resolve Armour Saves.

    5. Roll on Injury Table for each unsaved wound.

    6. Critical hits bypass armour saves.

    7. No wound pooling. Each model resolves attacks individually.

    Injury

    Roll 1D6 per unsaved wound:

    D6 Result
    1–2 Knocked Down
    3–4 Stunned
    5–6 Out of Action

    Multi-wound models: Ignore the first Out of Action result — instead lose one Wound. At 0 Wounds, Out of Action results apply normally. Weapons causing multiple wounds: +1 to Injury roll.

    Status effects: - Knocked Down — may stand during Movement Phase. - Stunned — becomes Knocked Down at start of next Recovery Phase. - Out of Action — removed from play. Counts toward Rout threshold.

    Rout

    • Rout threshold: 25% or more of starting models are Out of Action.
    • At the end of each turn the threshold is met, take a Rout Test.
    • Roll 2D6 — if result exceeds the Leader's Ld, the warband routs. Battle ends immediately.
    • A warband that has reached the threshold may voluntarily withdraw at the start of its Rout Phase with no additional penalty.

    Terrain & Verticality

    Action Test On Failure
    Climb (up to M") Initiative test Model falls
    Jump (up to I") Initiative test Model falls
    Death from Above (Diving Charge) Initiative test Model falls

    Death from Above (success): +1 to hit in the first round of combat.

    Falling: Model suffers a hit (Mordheim falling rules). Armour saves apply. Survivor is placed Knocked Down.

    Difficult terrain: Costs double movement. Cannot Run through difficult terrain unless it fits within the movement allowance.
