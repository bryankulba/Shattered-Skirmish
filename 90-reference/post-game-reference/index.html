    SHATTERED – Post-Game Reference Sheet

    Post-Battle Sequence

    Resolve in this order after every battle.

    Step Action
    1 Determine Outcome — Resolve objectives. Routed warband loses.
    2 Injury Resolution — Roll on Injury Table for every model taken Out of Action.
    3 Secure Resource Caches — Caches held by surviving/withdrawing models are Secured. Others are lost.
    4 Resource Conversion — Per Secured Cache: roll 1D3 → gain that many RP. Gain 1 Salvage. RP from this step cannot be spent this sequence.
    5 Repair & Refit — Spend Salvage now. Unspent Salvage is retained.
    6 Exploration — Roll Exploration dice. Resolve results.
    7 Recruitment — If a Recruitment Opportunity is available, spend 5 RP and roll on the New Recruit Table, or decline.
    8 Experience & Advancement — Award XP. Resolve advancements.
    9 Update Roster — Record everything. Prepare for next battle.

    Injury Resolution

    Roll for every model taken Out of Action. Apply results immediately.

    Hero Injury Table — roll 2D6

    2D6 Result
    2 Dead — Remove from roster.
    3 Captured/Missing — Miss next battle. Returns with Armour Compromised if warband controls field next game; otherwise removed.
    4 Multiple Trauma — Roll twice (re-roll Dead). Apply both.
    5 Armour Compromised — –1 Armour Save until repaired (2 Salvage).
    6 Weapon Damaged — One weapon gains Damaged until repaired (1 Salvage).
    7 Full Recovery — No lasting effects.
    8 Concussion — –1 Initiative next battle only.
    9 Crippling Wound — –1 Movement permanently.
    10 Oath of Moment — Choose a named foe; +1 Ld on Rout tests and re-roll one failed To Hit per battle against them.
    11 Bionic Replacement Needed — –1 WS until repaired or stabilised.
    12 Hardened by Fire — +1 XP immediately. Re-roll the next Advance roll.

    Henchman Injury Table — roll 2D6

    2D6 Result
    2 Dead — Remove from roster.
    3 Lost in the Ruins — Miss next battle. Returns with No Weaponry if warband wins next game; otherwise removed.
    4 Deep Wounds — Miss next battle. Returns with Armour Compromised.
    5 Armour Compromised — –1 Armour Save until repaired.
    6 Weapon Damaged — One weapon becomes Damaged until repaired.
    7 Full Recovery — No lasting effects.
    8 Shaken — –1 Leadership next battle only.
    9 Battle Scar — –1 Initiative permanently.
    10 Eye Damage — –1 BS until repaired or stabilised.
    11 Machine-Flesh — –1 Movement next battle only, then recovers.
    12 Veteran Instincts — +1 XP immediately.

    Salvage Spending

    Salvage may only be spent during Step 5: Repair & Refit.

    Action Cost
    Repair Armour Compromised 2 Salvage
    Repair Damaged Weapon 1 Salvage
    Re-equip Weaponless Recruit 1 Salvage
    Stabilise Injury (re-roll one non-permanent result; must accept second) 1 Salvage

    Salvage cannot improve characteristics, purchase weapons, or recruit models.

    Exploration

    Resolved during Step 6.

    Roll Exploration dice: - 1D6 per surviving Hero. - +1D6 if the warband controlled the field. - If more than 3 dice are rolled, retain only the three highest.

    Resolving results: 1. Check retained dice for duplicates. 2. If duplicates exist → use Duplicate Table (overrides Total Table). 3. If all unique → add the three dice and use Total Table. 4. If any retained die shows a natural 6 → gain 1 Recruitment Opportunity (one per sequence).

    Total Table (all unique dice — add results)

    Total Result
    6 Scorched Rubble — 1 Salvage.
    7 Broken Armour Plates — 2 Salvage.
    8 Recovered Ammunition — 1D3 RP.
    9 Battlefield Debris — 1 Salvage, 1 RP.
    10 Partial Supply Crate — 1D3 RP, 1 Salvage.
    11 Field Medicae Station — Re-roll one Injury result (must accept second). 1 Salvage.
    12 Sealed Locker — 1D3 RP, 2 Salvage.
    13 Strategic Data Core — 2 RP. One Hero +1 XP.
    14 Hardened Supply Vault — 2D3 RP, 1 Salvage.
    15 Hidden Arsenal — 2D3 RP, 2 Salvage.

    Duplicate Table

    Roll Result
    Double 1 Collapsed Substructure — 1 Salvage.
    Double 2 Scattered Munitions — 1D3 RP.
    Double 3 Damaged Vox Relay — 1 Salvage. Re-roll one retained die before resolving further duplicates.
    Double 4 Fallen Legionary — 2 Salvage.
    Double 5 Armoury Fragments — 1D3 RP, 1 Salvage.
    Double 6 Legion Distress Signal — 1 Recruitment Opportunity.
    Triple 1 Rad-Saturated Vault — One random model suffers S4 hit. 1 Salvage.
    Triple 2 Munitorum Depot — 2D3 RP.
    Triple 3 Tactical Intelligence Cache — 2 RP. One Hero +1 XP.
    Triple 4 Secure Armoury — 2 Salvage, 1D3 RP.
    Triple 5 Mechanicum Stockpile — 3 Salvage.
    Triple 6 Shattered Legion Survivor — 1 Recruitment Opportunity, 1 Salvage.
    Quadruple 2D3 RP, 2 Salvage.
    Five of a Kind 3D3 RP, 3 Salvage, 1 Recruitment Opportunity.

    Recruitment

    Resolved during Step 7. Requires a Recruitment Opportunity and an open warband slot.

    • Pay the full RP cost of the model type.
    • Roll 2D6 on the Recruitment Table.
    • Roll 2D6 on the Allegiance Table.
    • Apply all results immediately.

    Recruits begin with 0 XP. Must follow all Warband Creation equipment restrictions.

    Recruitment Table — roll 2D6

    2D6 Result
    2 Deranged Survivor — +1 Attack in first round of combat each battle. Roll 1D6 at battle start: on 1, must move toward nearest visible enemy first activation.
    3 Armour Scarred — Begins with Armour Compromised.
    4 Weaponless Arrival — Begins with No Weaponry. Must be re-equipped with Salvage.
    5 Shaken Survivor — –1 Leadership next battle only.
    6 Line Legionary — No additional effects.
    7 Recovered Brother — No additional effects.
    8 Disciplined Fighter — +1 XP immediately.
    9 Proven Under Fire — +1 XP.
    10 Recovered Wargear — 1 Salvage.
    11 Veteran of Attrition — +2 XP.
    12 Unbroken Astartes — +1 XP and +1 Ld on Rout tests while operational.

    Allegiance Table — roll 2D6

    2D6 Result
    2 Open Hostility — –1 Ld permanently. Warband must re-roll first successful Rout test each battle while this model is operational.
    3 Distrusted — Cannot benefit from Ld re-rolls from Veteran Sergeant.
    4 Tense Alliance — Narrative only.
    5 Uneasy Cohesion — –1 Ld first battle only.
    6 Conditional Loyalty — No additional effect.
    7 Accepted into the Line — No additional effect.
    8 Earned Respect — +1 XP immediately.
    9 Trusted Brother — Warband may re-roll one failed Rout test per campaign while this model is operational.
    10 Shared Oath — Recruit and one Hero both gain +1 XP.
    11 Battle-Bound — +1 Ld while within 6" of the Veteran Sergeant.
    12 Oath-Sworn to the Shattered — Ignores the first failed Rout test each battle.

    Experience & Advancement

    XP Awards (per battle)

    Condition XP
    Survive the battle +1
    Take an enemy Out of Action +1
    Scenario contribution +1 (max once per battle)
    Warband controls the field (Veteran Sergeant only) +1

    Maximum +3 XP per model per battle.

    Advancement Roll

    When a model reaches an advancement threshold, roll 3D6 on the Advancement Table.

    • Results of 3, 12, or 18 → roll 1D6 on the Characteristic Table instead.
    • If a result cannot be applied, re-roll.
    • Each characteristic increase may only be gained once per model.

    Advancement Table — roll 3D6

    3D6 Result
    4 Relentless Advance — May move and fire non-Assault weapons without penalty.
    5 Close-Quarters Killer — Re-roll one failed To Wound in melee per battle.
    6 Steady Under Fire — Ignore the first failed Ammo roll each battle.
    7 Oath of Moment — Nominate one enemy at battle start. Re-roll one failed To Hit per round against that model.
    8 Grim Determination — May ignore being Knocked Down once per battle.
    9 Brutal Charge — +1 Strength in the first round of a charge.
    10 Heart of the Legion — If this model did not move this activation, +1 FP when firing.
    11 Precision Fire — Does not need to target the closest model when firing.
    13 Battlefield Calculus — Once per battle, after both sides roll for activation priority, may re-roll your result.
    14 Fearless Aspect — Causes Fear.
    15 Executioner — If this model takes an enemy Out of Action in melee, gain +1 XP (max once per battle).
    16 Battlefield Predator — +1 Attack in the first round of combat.
    17 Iron Resolve — Once per battle, ignore Stunned. Treat as Knocked Down instead.

    Characteristic Table — roll 1D6

    1D6 Result
    1 +1 Weapon Skill
    2 +1 Ballistic Skill
    3 +1 Initiative
    4 +1 Leadership
    5 +1 Attack
    6 +1 Wound

    If the rolled characteristic has already been increased for this model, re-roll.
