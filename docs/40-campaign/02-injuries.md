# Injuries

Injuries represent lasting degradation: ruptured armour seals, concussive trauma, nerve damage, and cumulative attrition.

Roll on the appropriate Injury Table for each model taken Out of Action.

Injury Resolution occurs during Step 2 of the Post-Battle Sequence.

---

## Hero Injury Table

Roll 2D6 for each Hero taken Out of Action.

| 2D6 | Result |
|------|--------|
| 2 | **Dead** – Remove from roster. |
| 3 | **Captured / Missing** – Miss next battle. If the warband controls the field next game, return with *Armour Compromised*. Otherwise removed. |
| 4 | **Multiple Trauma** – Roll twice on this table (re-roll Dead). Apply both results. |
| 5 | **Armour Compromised** – –1 Armour Save until repaired (2 Salvage). |
| 6 | **Weapon Damaged** – One weapon gains a persistent *Damaged* condition until repaired (1 Salvage). |
| 7 | **Full Recovery** – No lasting effects. |
| 8 | **Concussion** – –1 Initiative next battle only. |
| 9 | **Crippling Wound** – –1 Movement permanently. |
| 10 | **Oath of Moment** – Choose a named foe; gain +1 Leadership for Rout tests and re-roll one failed To Hit per battle against them. |
| 11 | **Bionic Replacement Needed** – –1 WS until repaired or stabilised. |
| 12 | **Hardened by Fire** – Gain +1 XP immediately and re-roll the next Advance roll. |

---

## Henchman Injury Table

Roll 2D6 for each Henchman taken Out of Action.

| 2D6 | Result |
|------|--------|
| 2 | **Dead** – Remove from roster. |
| 3 | **Lost in the Ruins** – Miss next battle. If the warband wins next game, return with *No Weaponry*. Otherwise removed. |
| 4 | **Deep Wounds** – Miss next battle. Return with *Armour Compromised*. |
| 5 | **Armour Compromised** – –1 Armour Save until repaired. |
| 6 | **Weapon Damaged** – One weapon becomes *Damaged* until repaired. |
| 7 | **Full Recovery** – No lasting effects. |
| 8 | **Shaken** – –1 Leadership next battle only. |
| 9 | **Battle Scar** – –1 Initiative permanently. |
| 10 | **Eye Damage** – –1 BS until repaired or stabilised. |
| 11 | **Machine-Flesh** – –1 Movement next battle only, then recovers. |
| 12 | **Veteran Instincts** – Gain +1 XP immediately. |

---

## Injury Persistence

- Permanent effects remain until repaired or otherwise resolved.
- Temporary effects expire as stated.
- Models removed from the roster are permanently lost.

All injury results must be recorded immediately.
