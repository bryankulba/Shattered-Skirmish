# Special Rules
Special Rules associated with weapons as per AOD 3.0 rules are assummed to not be applicable in Shattered unless noted below (with or without modification).
 
 ---

## Breaching (x)
Breaching (X) lowers the die result required to score a Critical Hit. Critical Hits bypass the armour save.

Normally, a Critical Hit is scored on a natural roll of 6 when rolling to wound.

If a weapon has Breaching (X), a Critical Hit is instead scored on a natural roll of X or higher.

Examples:

Breaching (6) – Critical Hit on 5 or 6

Breaching (5+) – Critical Hit on 4, 5, or 6

Breaching (4+) – Critical Hit on 3, 4, 5, or 6


---

## Shred(X)
Shred (X) represents weapons that tear through flesh and internal systems, causing catastrophic damage when they strike cleanly.

If the attacker rolls a natural X or higher when rolling to wound, the hit Shreds the target.

A Shredding hit adds +1 to the Injury roll if the target is taken Out of Action.

The target may still take Armour Saves normally before any Injury roll is made.
