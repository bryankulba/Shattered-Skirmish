# Warband Creation

Warbands are created using Requisition Points (RP).

Each model is purchased individually.

Warbands are not units and never qualify for unit-based rules or scaling unlocks.

---

## Starting Requisition

Recommended starting value:

- 100 RP

All models and upgrades must be paid for using RP.

Unspent RP may be retained for campaign use.

---

## Composition Structure

Warbands are divided into:

- **Heroes**
- **Henchmen**

### Mandatory Model

- Exactly 1 Veteran Sergeant

A warband may not begin play without a Veteran Sergeant.

---

## Composition Limits

A warband may include:

**Heroes**
- 1 Veteran Sergeant (mandatory)
- 0–2 Veteran Marines

**Henchmen**
- 0–3 Tactical Marines (10 RP each)
- 0–3 Despoiler Marines (10 RP each)
- 0–2 Breacher Marines (12 RP each)
- 0–2 Tactical Support Marines (8 RP each)

These limits apply at all times.

The Age of Darkness scaling rule “For every five models in the unit…” does not apply in SHATTERED.

---

## Model Costs

**Heroes**
- Veteran Sergeant — 15 RP
- Veteran Marine — 15 RP

**Henchmen**
- Tactical Marine — 10 RP
- Despoiler Marine — 10 RP
- Breacher Marine — 12 RP
- Tactical Support Marine — 8 RP

RP must be paid in full when recruiting a model.

RP is not refunded unless explicitly stated elsewhere.

---

## Baseline Equipment

All models receive their default equipment as listed in the appropriate Age of Darkness Liber books at no additional RP cost.

Veteran Sergeants and Veteran Marines must begin with either:

- A Bolter  
- A Bolt Pistol  

They may not have both.

Exceptions:

- Tactical Marines do not receive Bolt Pistols.
- Breacher Marines do not receive Bolt Pistols.

Baseline equipment must be recorded on the roster.

---

## Weapon Selection Rules

### Veteran Sergeant

May select upgrades from:

- Legion officer wargear
- Legion combi-weapons

---

### Veteran Marines

May select upgrades from:

- Sergeant melee weapons
- Legion pistols

May upgrade to:

- Astartes shotgun
- Disintegrator rifle
- Volkite charger

---

### Tactical Support Marines

May select one special weapon from the approved Special Weapons list.

No other upgrades permitted.

---

### Tactical Marines, Despoilers, and Breachers

- No upgrade options.
- No additional wargear selections permitted.
- May only use baseline equipment.

---

## Equipment Restrictions

The following are not permitted:

- Heavy weapons
- Terminator armour or Terminator weapons
- Jump packs
- Psykers
- Techmarines
- Apothecaries
- Sponson, pintle, or vehicle-mounted weapons

Warbands never qualify for:

- Unit-based unlocks
- Scaling equipment allowances
- “For every five models in the unit…” rules

---

## Roster Requirements

All models, equipment, and upgrades must be recorded on the warband roster.

A warband must remain within composition limits at all times.
