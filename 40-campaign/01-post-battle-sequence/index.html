    Post-Battle Sequence

    Resolve the following steps in order after each battle.

    This order is mandatory unless a scenario explicitly states otherwise.

    1. Determine Battle Outcome

    • Resolve scenario objectives.
    • Determine which warband controlled the field, if applicable.
    • A warband that Routed is considered to have lost.
    • Apply any scenario-specific post-battle effects.

    2. Injury Resolution

    • Roll on the appropriate Injury Table for every model taken Out of Action.
    • Apply all permanent and temporary injuries immediately.
    • Remove models that are killed or rendered unfit for service.

    This step defines the warband’s true post-battle state.

    3. Secure Resource Caches

    • Determine which Resource Caches were secured.
    • Caches carried by surviving or withdrawing models are Secured.
    • Unsecured caches are lost.
    • Record the number of Secured Caches.

    4. Resource Conversion

    For each Secured Resource Cache:

    • Roll 1D3 and gain that many Requisition Points (RP).
    • Gain 1 Salvage.

    RP gained during this step may not be spent during this sequence.

    Record all totals on the warband roster.

    5. Repair & Refit

    • Spend Salvage to repair armour, weapons, or stabilise injuries.
    • Apply all effects immediately.
    • Salvage not spent is retained.

    Salvage may only be spent during this step.

    6. Exploration

    • Roll Exploration dice as defined in the Exploration section.
    • Resolve all Exploration results.

    If one or more Exploration dice show a 6, the warband may gain one Recruitment Opportunity, if eligible.

    7. Recruitment

    If a Recruitment Opportunity is available, the warband may:

    • Spend 5 RP and roll on the New Recruit Table, or
    • Decline the opportunity.

    Unused Recruitment Opportunities expire at the end of this sequence.

    8. Experience & Advancement

    • Award Experience Points (XP).
    • Resolve advancements, if any.

    Advancement follows the Mordheim Advance Roll system unless explicitly overridden.

    9. Update Roster

    • Record all injuries, repairs, RP totals, Salvage totals, XP gains, advancements, and roster changes.
    • Prepare the warband for the next battle.
